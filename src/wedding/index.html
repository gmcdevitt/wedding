Our Love Story

Our story began in September of 2021 when we first officially met at the Polish Festival through mutual friends. In January of 2022 Gerard came to a Trivia night (that weeks theme was Harry Potter) that Storm went to regularly with mutual friends.

Over the next few months they bonded and became friends while attending many events together (trivia nights, concerts, friend hang outs, etc). They quickly became close friends during this time.

Fast forward to summer/fall of 2023 when that friendship became more. They both realized that the feelings they had for each other were worth risking the friendship over. Almost a year later and Gerard decided being in a relationship was not enough, he wanted to upgrade the title of boyfriend to fiancé!

We truly appreciate all of your love and support over the last few years and are so grateful to give you all a night of fun, food, dancing, and drinks - which hopefully none of us will ever forget.

With Love, S & G
