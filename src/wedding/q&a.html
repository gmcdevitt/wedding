Are kids allowed?

While we adore your children, we kindly request that our wedding remain a child-free celebration.

What is the attire?

Black Tie optional. The wedding party will be wearing plum, burgundy and black, so we kindly ask guests to avoid these colors to let them shine. Thank you for understanding!

Will there be parking onsite?

Yes, there is parking onsite (60 car maximum) and the venue provides parking attendants starting one hour before event time. Vehicles may not be left overnight, ride share services are encouraged if you plan on drinking during the reception.

Do I get to bring a plus one?

We have carefully planned our day around celebrating with only our closest family and friends; thus, only guests who are named on the invitation are able to attend. We appreciate your understanding on the matter.

Will the ceremony be indoor or outdoor?

At this time our ceremony is planned for being outdoors, but this is weather permitting. If the weather looks to be too cold or rainy our venue has an indoor ceremony space that will accommodate our guests!

Should I bring cash for the bar?

We are offering an open bar during Cocktail Hour and the Reception with wine, two specialty cocktails and beer available. Tipping the bar tender is not required, but always appreciated by the staff!

Is the venue handicap accessible?

Yes, the venue is ADA compliant. The cocktail hour will be served downstairs and the reception upstairs. There are also restrooms on both levels for our guests to use.

When should I arrive at the venue?

We ceremony begins at 4 PM, and access to the venue for guests opens an hour before (3PM). We recommend showing up at least 30 minutes early to ensure that you don't miss the ceremony! Guests who arrive late are encouraged to either wait for the procession to complete before finding a seat in the back, or wait in the cocktail lounge until the ceremony is over.

Are there any restrictions for the venue?

Use of the farmhouse, pool, and surrounding area is not permitted. The horse barn and any designated horse area (pasture, training area, feed room, etc.) is prohibited from use and guest access.
